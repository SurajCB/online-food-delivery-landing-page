    About Us

    At Nammura Breakfast Makers, we know that a good day begins with a good breakfast. Founded by Suraj C B and Preetham T, our dream is simple — to bring you the same homely flavors we grew up with. Every crispy dosa, fluffy idli, and steaming cup of filter coffee is made to remind you of the comfort of home and the joy of sharing food with family. We’re not just serving breakfast; we’re serving memories, warmth, and a taste of tradition made specially for you. Because here, your mornings matter to us as much as they do to you..
